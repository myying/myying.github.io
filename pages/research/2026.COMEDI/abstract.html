COpernicus Marine services with Enhanced Data assimilation and improved Interoperability (COMEDI) is an European HORIZON project with partners from the 7 ocean Monitoring and Forecasting Centers (MFCs). The objective is to modernize and harmonize DA practices across all MFCs enabling them to leverage new technologies and utilize new types of ocean observations to achieve better accuracy and reliability.

COMEDI will enhance each MFC's DA software with novel DA methods; offer new capabilities to assimilate new types of satellite observations; improve MFC's interoperability by establishing Joint Development Teams and devloping interoperable tools; and demonstrate the use of innovations in real-MFC operational context and engage CMEMS stakeholders, ensuring that CMEMS users can fully benefit from these advancements.
